Instructions:

The ship follows the mouse. Left click to fire a laser. Right click to fire a bomb. Ammo is unlimited but overheating decreases the fire rate.

Just try to live as you can, there's no objective yet.

Contributions are very welcome. To see the code repository go to https://bitbucket.org/stothardj/tempus

This is written in coffeescript and run through the closure compiler (simple_optimizations)

The background music was stolen from http://www.lukhash.com
